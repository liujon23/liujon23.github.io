About Me

Hi! Welcome to my page. My name is Jonathan Liu, and I'm an undergraduate studying Computer Science and Mathematics at UC Berkeley.

My research interests lie in Algorithmic Game Theory. I am currently doing research with Professor Carlo Séquin on optimizing discrete knots (I should probably put that somewhere huh), and I'm also the President of UC Berkeley's Undergraduate Theory CS club. In my free time, I play tennis and trick-taking games of all kinds (but mostly bridge).
